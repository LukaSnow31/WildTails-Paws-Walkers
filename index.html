Welcome to WildTails & Paws Walkers

Every dog deserves adventure, understanding, and a dedicated friend. Whether it's a hike through the woods or a gentle stroll, we tailor every walk to match your dog's needs.

Our services run 24/7—including weekends and holidays—and we happily accept last-minute, same-day, and emergency bookings. No matter the weather, we’re out and about, with special plans in place for those extra soggy days, so your dog never misses out!

Care at the Heart of Every Walk and Sitting

Specialist One-to-One

We prefer to focus on each dog individually to fulfil their every need.

Adventure Walks

Long-distance nature walks that provide exercise and mental stimulation.

Special Needs Dogs

We welcome dogs that need extra understanding, care, and support.

Behavioural Challenges

We work with nervous, reactive, and misunderstood dogs patiently and compassionately.

Training Add-Ons

Short training sessions built into walks to build confidence and good habits.
