Welcome to WildTails & Paws Walkers

Your dog’s next adventure starts here!

From peaceful woodland strolls to energy-burning trail hikes, we create walks that suit your dog’s pace, personality, and needs.

We are available day or night, in all weather conditions, offering flexible walking, sitting, and small pet care services every day of the year.

Emergency and same-day bookings are always welcome!

Our services extend beyond dogs. We also provide dedicated cat sitting and small animal care tailored to each pet’s needs.

Whether you have a cat, guinea pig, rabbit, hamster, or other small companion, we ensure attentive, compassionate care in the comfort of their home.

This includes feeding, fresh water, litter tray or cage cleaning, playtime, and lots of affection. We always adapt to your pet’s routine, temperament, and preferences.

Because every companion deserves more than basic care — they deserve the WildTails experience.
