About WildTails & Paws Walkers

Founded in 2024, WildTails & Paws Walkers began with a heartfelt mission: to provide dedicated, compassionate care tailored to the unique needs of each pet. With over a decade of hands-on experience, our team understands animals of all kinds—from playful puppies to retired racehorses with special needs.

We know every pet is an individual, with their own personality and sensitivities. From energetic pups to nervous rescues, reserved seniors, and even our feline and small mammal friends, our approach is grounded in empathy, patience, and personalised support.

Initially, we worked through third-party platforms like Rover. But we wanted to offer transparent pricing, direct communication, and truly personalised care. Operating independently allows us to build stronger, meaningful relationships with both pets and their owners.

"Every pet deserves kindness, respect, and patience. We’re not just carers—we’re trusted partners in your pet’s happiness and wellbeing."

From woodland walks to quiet afternoons of companionship, we provide tailored adventures that keep pets happy, healthy, and loved. One animal at a time, one meaningful moment at a time.
