About WildTails & Paws Walkers

Founded in 2024, WildTails & Paws Walkers began with a simple mission: to provide dedicated, affordable, and compassionate care for dogs of all breeds, ages, and temperaments.

With over 10 years of hands-on experience working with animals, our background includes not only canine care, but also managing retired racehorses with various disabilities.

This foundation gave us deep insight into animal behavior, patience, and the importance of tailored, respectful handling.

We first began offering services through platforms like Rover, but the high fees made it difficult to maintain fair, transparent pricing.

So we took the leap into independence to provide quality care without compromise, and build stronger, more personal connections with our clients.

Whether your dog is energetic, anxious, elderly, reactive, or simply misunderstood we’re here to walk beside them.

One dog at a time. One walk at a time
