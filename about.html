About WildTails & Paws Walkers

WildTails & Paws Walkers was founded in 2024 with a heartfelt mission to provide trusted, compassionate, and affordable pet care for all animals, regardless of breed, age, temperament, or behavioural needs. The inspiration came from our own struggles to find suitable care for our two huskies while travelling. Many services were either unavailable or too costly, which motivated us to build a dependable, home-style business that offered exceptional care without compromise.

Before founding WildTails & Paws Walkers, we spent years working hands-on with animals of all kinds, including retired racehorses, birds of prey, and wildlife in conservation programs. These experiences gave us a strong foundation in animal welfare, behaviour, and trust-building—skills that continue to guide our approach to every pet we care for today.

Our personal journey with our huskies has deepened our understanding of canine behaviour, training, and emotional wellbeing. Through patience, consistency, and compassion, we’ve learned how to connect with animals from all backgrounds—whether they’re energetic pups, nervous rescues, or gentle seniors.

At WildTails & Paws Walkers, we believe every animal deserves kindness, respect, and individual attention. From adventurous woodland walks to calm moments of companionship, we provide a tailored experience that ensures each pet feels safe, loved, and understood. Our business is built on trust, transparency, and a shared passion for helping pets live their happiest, healthiest lives—one paw at a time.
