About WildTails & Paws Walkers

Founded in 2024, WildTails & Paws Walkers was born from a simple but heartfelt challenge, finding trustworthy, affordable care for our own huskies while we were away. After struggling to find services that met our dogs’ needs and fit within a realistic budget, we were inspired to create something better: a business built on empathy, accessibility, and genuine love for animals.

Before WildTails began, we had years of hands-on experience caring for a wide variety of animals from retired racehorses and birds of prey to supporting wildlife conservation programs. These experiences gave us deep insight into animal behaviour, trust-building, and overall wellbeing knowledge we continue to apply every day in our pet care services.

Our goal has always been clear: to provide a safe, welcoming, and inclusive service for all pets no matter their breed, age, temperament, or behavioural challenges. Every dog, cat, or small pet in our care receives individual attention and a personalised experience that supports their unique personality and needs.

We initially began through third-party platforms such as Rover but soon realised the importance of offering transparent pricing, direct communication, and fully tailored care. Operating independently allows us to build stronger, more personal relationships with both pets and their owners creating trust that lasts well beyond the first walk.

"Every pet deserves kindness, respect, and patience. We’re not just carers, we’re trusted partners in your pet’s happiness and wellbeing."

From adventurous woodland walks to quiet afternoons of companionship, WildTails & Paws Walkers is more than a service, it’s a promise. A promise to care for every animal as if they were our own, one moment and one pawprint at a time.
