Our Services & Pricing

We provide safe, tailored dog walking and sitting for all types of dogs — from energetic pups to reactive or high-needs companions. Here's our full breakdown:

Reactive, Aggressive or High-Needs Dogs (per dog)

Dog Sitting, Day care and Drop ins (per dog)

  • Dog overnight sitting: £25/per night and per dog

Cat Sitting (per cat)

Small Pet Care
