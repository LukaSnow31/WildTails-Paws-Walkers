This includes (but is not limited to): housemates, family members, guests, and maintenance personnel.

Failure to disclose this information may result in immediate cancellation of the booking without refund.

2. Undisclosed Persons Policy

If an undisclosed individual is present at the property, we reserve the right to:

  • Leave immediately if we feel unsafe

We reserve the right to keep any dog on a lead if necessary.
